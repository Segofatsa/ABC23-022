DEAD BOY DETECTIVES

From Emmy® nominee Steve Yockey (The Flight Attendant, Supernatural), Doom Patrol's Jeremy Carver and Berlanti Productions, the DC drama DEAD BOY DETECTIVES is a horror detective series born from DC's The Sandman and Dead Boy Detectives comics. It's a fresh take on a ghost story that explores loss, grief, and death through the lens of Edwin Payne and Charles Rowland, two dead British teenagers, and their very alive friend, Crystal Palace. So, it's a lot like a vintage detective series - only darker and on acid.
